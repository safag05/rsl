📋 2026 League Format

  • 🎯 Goal: Get together, compete, and have fun — this league is for everyone!
  • 📅 Regular Season: 8 games per team, round-robin format.
  • 🏅 After the regular season, teams split into Division A (Top 4) and Division B (5th–8th) for playoffs.

🏆 Playoff Format — 2 Weeks

🔵 Division A — Top 4

Week 1 · Semifinals
  • 1st vs 4th
  • 2nd vs 3rd
Week 2 · Finals
  • 🥇 Winners → Championship
  • 🥉 Losers → 3rd Place Game

⚪ Division B — 5th–8th

Week 1 · Semifinals
  • 5th vs 8th
  • 6th vs 7th
Week 2 · Finals
  • 🥇 Winners → Div B Championship
  • 🥉 Losers → 3rd Place Game
🏆 Div A & B Champions get custom trophies 🥇🥈🥉 Top 3 in each division get medals
